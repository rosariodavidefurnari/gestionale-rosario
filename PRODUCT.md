# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Rosario Furnari is the primary operator. He uses the CRM as a working console to
manage clients, contacts, projects, services, quotes, invoices, payments,
expenses, fiscal checks and AI-assisted import. The interface is used during
real operational work, often to verify money, fiscal data and project state
quickly without guessing.

## Product Purpose

Gestionale Rosario Furnari is an internal CRM and fiscal operations tool for a
small creative business. Success means the system preserves one deterministic
source of truth for clients, projects, work logs, emitted and received invoices,
cash, pending payments and fiscal obligations. The UI must help the operator
understand what is true now, take the next action, and avoid fiscal or cash
mistakes.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, operational. The product should feel like a trustworthy business
console, not a marketing site, a decorative dashboard or a generic SaaS demo.
It should make high-risk financial states obvious, reduce cognitive load and
respect repeated daily use.

## Anti-references

Do not use landing-page composition, oversized hero sections, decorative card
grids, badge-heavy explanations, hidden critical details, gradient decoration or
visual novelty that competes with the task. Do not make money or fiscal state
depend on vague labels, tooltip-only meaning or desktop-only affordances.

## Design Principles

1. Deterministic first: every important value must have a clear source and a
   visible operational meaning.
2. Fiscal clarity beats clever UI: invoices, cash, taxes and pending payments
   must be readable without interpretation games.
3. Operational account and fiscal recipient are distinct when the domain says
   so; the interface must show the distinction only where it helps the task.
4. Desktop and mobile must preserve the same financial truth, even when layout
   density changes.
5. Use familiar product UI patterns and existing components before inventing
   new interaction models.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA behavior on product surfaces: keyboard-reachable
controls, visible focus, sufficient contrast, semantic labels, no color-only
meaning for state, reduced-motion-friendly interactions and responsive layouts
that do not overlap or truncate critical fiscal text.
